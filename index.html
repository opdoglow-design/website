Happiest Birthday To My Beautiful Future Wife (Maybe) 💖

First Of All, Thank You. I know you are thinking why I am saying thank you in a birthday wish message. Let me explain that to you...

It has been like 4 months since we met for the first time. At that time, I never thought that I would fall for you that badly. Maybe it was written in my fate, that's why I fell for you.

You came into my life at a time when I was feeling very lonely. I even forgot how to smile. But after starting to talk with you, I again learned how to smile. You brought the smile back on my face...

I don't know what's special about you, but when I talk with you, I feel like I am the happiest man on Earth. And really, I don't know how you do that... But somehow you always make my day better just by talking with me.

Sometimes I think about how randomly we met. It feels so unreal... Out of so many people in this world, I ended up meeting someone like you.

Maybe it's just luck or... maybe it's something more beautiful than that.

I know sometimes I joke around and call you my future wife, but I truly care about you more than words can explain.

You are one of the most amazing people I have ever met—your kindness, the way you talk (I know you are very quarrelsome, but I still like your behavior)... Everything about you feels very special to me.

I am really thankful that you came into my life and stayed these past few months. Talking to you has been some of the happiest moments of my life.

So on your special day, I just want to say—I hope your life is filled with happiness and beautiful moments. You truly deserve all the good things in the world.

And if my fate is good enough, I want to be part of your future, part of your smile and dreams, and many more birthdays to come...

Once again, happiest birthday to the most beautiful girl I know in the world.

My main intention in this message is to tell you how special you are for me ❤️
